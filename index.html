Our Mission

At Guardian 4 Heroes, we are dedicated to supporting the mental wellness of our veterans and first responders in the Sauk Centre, Minnesota area through compassionate and empowering equine-facilitated psychotherapy (EFPT). We provide a safe, therapeutic environment to help heal PTSD, TBI, anxiety, and more through meaningful human-animal bonds.

What is Equine Therapy?

Equine-facilitated psychotherapy (EFP) is a type of therapy that involves interactions between patients and horses. It can be used to treat a variety of mental health conditions, including anxiety, depression, trauma, and behavioral issues. Horses are intuitive animals that can provide unique insights into human emotions and behaviors, making them powerful partners in the therapeutic process.

The Healing Power of Horses

Working with horses can offer profound physical and emotional benefits. It can help individuals develop trust, build confidence, improve communication skills, and process difficult emotions in a safe and supportive environment. The unique connection formed with horses can lead to significant breakthroughs in therapy.
