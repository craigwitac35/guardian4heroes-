Welcome

Welcome to Guardian 4 Heroes, where healing and growth meet the power of the horse. Our nonprofit is dedicated to equine-facilitated psychotherapy and assisted training, offering a compassionate and transformative experience for individuals seeking emotional, mental, and personal well-being.

Empowering Journeys Begin

Discover the transformative power of EFPT at Guardian 4 Heroes. Our programs harness the power of horses to support mental health and physical well-being. Join us on a journey of healing and growth.

A Nurturing Environment

Our facility in Sauk Centre provides a peaceful and safe space where veterans and first responders can connect with horses and each other. The serene environment is key to fostering healing and personal growth.
