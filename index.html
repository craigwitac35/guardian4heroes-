Welcome

Welcome to Guardian 4 Heroes, where healing and growth meet the power of the horse. Our nonprofit is dedicated to equine-facilitated psychotherapy and assisted training, offering a compassionate and transformative experience for individuals seeking emotional, mental, and personal well-being.

Through the unique connection between humans and horses, we provide a safe, supportive space for therapy, learning, and self-discovery.

Our Story & Mission

At Guardian 4 Heroes, we support the mental wellness of our veterans through innovative, compassionate, and empowering equine-facilitated psychotherapy-assisted training (EFPT). Founded by Sara Peterson, our mission is to honor and uplift heroes through the transformative power of the human-animal bond.

What is EFPT?

Equine Facilitated Psychotherapy Assisted Training (EFPT) is a therapeutic approach that uses horse interaction to help individuals rebuild emotional strength and social connection. Horses mirror human emotion and help participants explore trust, confidence, and communication in a safe, intuitive way.

Key Benefits of EFPT

Develops awareness and respect for personal boundaries through direct interaction with horses, who respond to clear, consistent cues.

Builds confidence and self-worth as participants achieve milestones and experience growth through their work with the horse.

Strengthens self-assurance by mastering horsemanship skills and overcoming challenges in a supportive environment.

Fosters connections with others who share similar experiences, creating a sense of camaraderie and mutual support.

Establishes a trusting, empathetic bond with horses, which can be therapeutic and help individuals understand non-verbal communication.

Develops practical skills in caring for and working with horses, fostering a sense of accomplishment and responsibility.

Empowering Lives Through Connection

Our Equine Facilitated Psychotherapy and Learning programs focus on building trust, love, and emotional well-being. Through powerful horse-human interactions, we foster healing, personal growth, and lasting transformation.
