Welcome

Welcome to Guardian 4 Heroes, where healing and growth meet the power of the horse. Our nonprofit is dedicated to equine-facilitated psychotherapy and assisted training, offering a compassionate and transformative experience for individuals seeking emotional, mental, and personal well-being.

Through the unique connection between humans and horses, we provide a safe, supportive space for therapy, learning, and self-discovery. Whether you're here for healing, training, or to support our mission, we're glad to have you with us.

Our Story & Mission

At Guardian 4 Heroes, we are dedicated to supporting the mental wellness of our veterans through innovative, compassionate, and empowering equine-facilitated psychotherapy-assisted training (EFPT). Our mission is to honor and uplift our heroes, guiding them towards healing, resilience, and a renewed sense of purpose through the transformative power of the human-animal bond.

Guardian 4 Heroes is owned and operated by Sara Peterson, a dedicated veteran advocate. We strive to create a safe, peaceful environment for mindful self-discovery, emotional awareness, and meaningful relationships with horses and each other.

What is EFPT?

Equine Facilitated Psychotherapy Assisted Training (EFPT) is a therapeutic approach using interactions with horses to help individuals reconnect with themselves and rebuild emotional and social skills. Horses are intuitive and responsive to human emotion, providing authentic, non-verbal feedback that promotes healing and trust.

Through EFPT, individuals can gain confidence, explore personal boundaries, and form strong bonds with both animals and people in their community.

Key Benefits of EFPT

  • Boundary Setting: Teaches clear, respectful communication
  • Self-Esteem: Builds confidence through success
  • Confidence Building: Master horsemanship and overcome fears
  • Friendship & Community: Connect with others on similar journeys
  • Bonding with Horses: Learn empathy through animal connection
  • Horsemanship Skills: Gain practical and emotional growth

Empowering Lives Through Connection

Our Equine Facilitated Psychotherapy and Learning programs focus on building trust, love, and emotional well-being. Through powerful horse-human interactions, we foster healing, personal growth, and lasting transformation.
