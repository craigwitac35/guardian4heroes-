Our Healing Programs

Holistic therapy for the body, mind, and spirit

Equine-Assisted Therapy

Connecting with horses to promote emotional regulation, confidence, and trust. Our equine program is a cornerstone of healing, especially for trauma and PTSD.

Sound Therapy & Meditation

Using sound bowls, nature, and guided sessions to relax the nervous system and create space for peace and introspection.

Yoga & Mindfulness

Movement, breathwork, and mindful presence taught by experienced instructors in peaceful settings that invite grounding and balance.

Community Circles

Weekly and monthly group gatherings for veterans and first responders to share, listen, and heal together in a safe space.
