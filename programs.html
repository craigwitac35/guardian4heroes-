Equine Facilitated Psychotherapy-Assisted Training (EFPT)

EFPT is at the core of what we do. Our programs use structured, interactive sessions with horses to help veterans and participants build self-awareness, manage emotions, and rediscover personal strength. Horses respond to human emotion with honest, non-verbal feedback — making them powerful allies in the healing process.

What We Offer

  • One-on-One Therapy Sessions:
    Personalized EFPT sessions tailored to each participant’s mental health needs, focusing on PTSD, anxiety, and emotional regulation.
  • Group Sessions:
    Peer-led and therapist-guided circles encouraging connection, trust, and shared healing among veterans and participants.
  • Horsemanship & Skill Building:
    Learn the basics of caring for and bonding with horses, building confidence, patience, and a sense of responsibility.
  • Community Events & Workshops:
    Educational and therapeutic events that offer opportunities for families, caregivers, and supporters to learn about EFPT and mental wellness.
  • Mindfulness & Self-Discovery:
    Quiet, reflective time with horses to explore emotions, boundaries, and inner healing.
